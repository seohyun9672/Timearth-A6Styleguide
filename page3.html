Borders

Both the check box and the time input button component have white (#FFFFFF) background, which would have no visibility without the stroke. To achieve higher discoverability of the component, 1px width with the colour of #6491E9 stroke is used.

Drop Shadow

Offset-X: 0 Offset-Y: 4px

Blur: 4px SpreadY: 0

Colour: #000000 Opacity: 25%

For the section container, the drop shadow is used to higher the level of contrast instead of a harsh line of stroke/outline.

The time result and the clickable button also adapt the drop shadow to create a depth to the component. It allows increased visibility and conveys interactability and clickability.

Roundness

Roundness is incorporated into all of our components, including buttons, boxes, and icons. It delivers the feeling of being safe, friendly, and approachable; thus, it helps to draw attention to content and to encourage interaction.

The corner radius varies between 5px to 10 px, depending on the size of the component, but it’s used accordingly to create rounded squares instead or fully rounded shapes.

Icons

The icons are imported from Font Awesome Icons. The edit icon and the hamburger menu icon use #000000 colour.

However, unlike those two icons, the social media icon uses our app’s highlight colour, which is #6491E9. If the edit and hamburger menu icons are intended to be looking blended with other fonts, the social media icon is our sub-CTA button, where the users are encouraged to share their results of the day.
