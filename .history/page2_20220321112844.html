Color Palette

Description of color palette

-OurAppName-'s color palette consists of muted tones of orange (#FF9042) and blue (#6B97ED), accompanied by white and black. The orange provides a sense of urgency and enthusiasm, and the blue provides a sense of stability and confidence. Overall, this color combination provides a reliable and attention-getting environment for the app.

Background, Menus, and Other Colored Elements

This app maintains a minimal look with a white background and a generous amount of whitespace. Colors in the color palette are used as ornamental background elements or button colors.

Major buttons throughout the app are the color “Orange Crayola”, #FF6C37 with text in the color “Indigo Dye”, #0D3B66. Secondary buttons are the color “Indigo Dye”, #0D3B66 with white (#ffffff) text.

Typography

-OurAppName- uses Nunito Sans as the typeface throughout the app. Nunito Sans is described as a "well-balanced" sans-serif font that includes a full set of weights, ranging from Extra Light to Black. This font was chosen for its ease of readability and balanced look, combined with its shorter and rounder build, following typography trends found in new apps. This adds a familiar, laid-back aura to contrast the vibrant and strong colors of the illustrations. All text throughout the app is in the color Black, #00000.
