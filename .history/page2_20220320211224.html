Typography

-OurAppName- uses Nunito Sans as the typeface throughout the app. Nunito Sans is described as a "well-balanced" sans-serif font that includes a full set of weights, ranging from Extra Light to Black. All text throughout the app is in the color "Indigo Dye", #0D3B66.
