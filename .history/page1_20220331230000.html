Logo

Timearth is founded to engage the world to take accountability for their ecological footprints and contribute to extending the Earth's lifespan.

All elements form the story we want to convey to people. An alarm clock represents that time is a limited resource and reminds people to start being more decisive in their world. The Earth inside an alarm clock symbolizes its time is running out and conveys the message that it's time to start doing something for the Earth. The logomark intentionally tilts slightly to the left to represent the urgency, pressure, and tension, conveying an idea that the Earth is running out of time.

Typography

Prompt is a loopless Thai, simple and geometric sans-serif typeface.
Its wide proportions and airy negative space enhance the readability and legibility of our logo.

Logo Variation

A stacked version of the logo will be Timearth's primary logo. This version will be used on the website header, marketing materials, etc. The vertical logo should always include the full business name and the logomark.

A horizontal version of the logo will be used where the vertical logo may not fit or be legible.

Logo Usage

Minimum Clear Space

To ensure our logo maintains legibility and maximize visibility, preserving a minimum clear space is required when used with other elements.

Minimum Sizes

Minimum size for the logo is required to maintain its impact and legibility. The logo should never appear smaller than the minimum size of 100x100 pixels.
