Logo

## is founded to engage the world to take accountability for their ecological footprints and contribute to extending the Earth's lifespan. Our logo itself tells people our story, brand, and ultimate purpose.
All elements form the story we want to convey to people. An alarm clock represents that time is a limited resource and reminds people to start being more decisive in their world. The Earth inside an alarm clock symbolizes its time is running out and conveys the message that it's time to start doing something for the Earth. The logomark intentionally tilts slightly to the left to represent the urgency, pressure, and tension, conveying an idea that the Earth is running out of time.
