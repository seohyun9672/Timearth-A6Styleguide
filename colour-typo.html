    Timearth's color palette consists of muted tones of orange (#FF9042) and blue (#6B97ED), accompanied by white and black. The orange provides a sense of urgency and enthusiasm, and the blue provides a sense of stability and confidence. Overall, this color combination provides a reliable and attention-getting environment for the app.

    Background

    This app maintains a minimal look with a white background and a generous amount of whitespace. Princeton Orange and Cornflower Blue are used as ornamental background elements, as shown on the left, or as button colors.

    Buttons

    There are three button colors within the app: Cornflower Blue, Princeton Orange and White. Major buttons are blue or white depending on the background graphics, and secondary buttons are orange. These colors were chosen to enhance contrast and emphasize calls to action. Button text is White (#FFFFFF) for colored buttons.

    Typography

    Timearth uses Nunito Sans as the typeface throughout the app. Nunito Sans is described as a "well-balanced" sans-serif font that includes a full set of weights, ranging from Extra Light to Black. This font was chosen for its ease of readability and balanced look, combined with its shorter and rounder build, following typography trends found in new apps. This adds a familiar, laid-back aura to contrast the vibrant and strong colors of the illustrations. All text throughout the app is in the color Black, #00000.
